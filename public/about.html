§ 01
Who we are

When visionary individuals unite,
extraordinary outcomes follow.

GCA is a non-profit, non-political global network dedicated to connecting scholars, professionals, and industry leaders worldwide. Humans are social by nature — our greatest ideas are rarely born in isolation.

At GCA, we believe that brilliant minds deserve to be connected, because when visionary individuals unite, extraordinary outcomes follow. Our mission is simple: to connect people across borders, cultures, and disciplines — turning meaningful dialogue into impactful actions that advances humanity.

We bridge the gap between rigorous scholarship and real-world practice — convening minds across journalism, public relations, digital media, linguistics, and intercultural communication to address the most urgent communicative challenges of our era.

GCA（Global Communication Association，国际交流协会）致力于汇聚全球学者、专业人士与行业领袖。人类生而具有社会属性——伟大的创想往往在群星闪耀时诞生。我们的使命很简单：打破地域、文化与领域的界限，将深刻对话转化为推动人类进步的切实行动。

§ 02 — On the ground

A chapter
made in Shenzhen.

Our chapter lives inside Shenzhen — one of the world's most dynamic cities for technology, design, and cross-cultural exchange. We run programs that step off campus on purpose, bringing students and professionals together in labs, galleries, studios, and the neighborhoods in between.

§ 03 — What a term looks like

A slow, steady cadence of gatherings.

An academic year of GCA is small on hype, long on hours spent together.

Week 01 — 03
I

Arrive & be known.

New members are matched with returning ones. First week is for introductions — not performance.

Monthly
II

Exchange nights.

One evening a month. A country, a cuisine, a story from home — hosted by members in rotation.

Quarterly
III

Speakers & panels.

Small-room talks with academics, journalists, and Shenzhen-based practitioners. Questions outnumber slides.

Term-end
IV

Into the city.

A closing program that takes the chapter off campus — a studio visit, a neighborhood walk, a shared meal.
